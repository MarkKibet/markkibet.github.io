Greek Ratatouille

Ingredients

  • 2 zucchinis, thinly sliced
  • 2 eggplants, thinly sliced
  • 2 bell peppers, thinly sliced
  • 4 tomatoes, thinly sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Step By Step Procedure

  1. Prepare the Vegetables
    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Thinly slice the zucchinis, eggplants, bell peppers, tomatoes, and red onion.
    • Mince the garlic cloves.
  2. Make the Sauce
    • In a small bowl, mix the olive oil, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and black pepper.
    • Stir until well combined.
  3. Layer the Vegetables
    • In a baking dish, arrange the sliced vegetables in alternating layers.
    • Drizzle the sauce evenly over the arranged vegetables.
  4. Bake the Ratatouille
    • Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and place it in the preheated oven.
    • Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Garnish and Serve
    • Remove the baking dish from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
    • Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.
